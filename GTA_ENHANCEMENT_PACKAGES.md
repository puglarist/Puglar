# GTA Enhancement Package Blueprint (50 Packages)

This blueprint proposes 50 package ideas focused on **graphics upgrades**, **monetization systems**, and **party chat via Meta Horizon integrations**.

## Graphics (20)
1. **Ultra Texture Streaming Pack** – on-demand 4K/8K texture tiles.
2. **PBR Material Overhaul** – physically based materials for world surfaces.
3. **Dynamic Global Illumination Pack** – probe + ray hybrid GI.
4. **Volumetric Weather FX** – storm/fog/cloud density simulation.
5. **Cinematic Color Grading Suite** – district-specific LUT pipelines.
6. **Character Skin Shader Revamp** – subsurface scattering upgrades.
7. **Vehicle Reflection Rewrite** – improved SSR + cube-map blending.
8. **Night City Neon Bounce** – emissive light propagation.
9. **Distance Detail Enhancer** – better LOD transitions and silhouettes.
10. **Crowd Animation Fidelity** – higher blend-tree quality for NPCs.
11. **Particle System v2** – GPU particles for debris/smoke/fire.
12. **Road Wetness & Puddle Simulation** – dynamic rain response.
13. **Destruction Debris Upgrade** – richer fracture and debris persistence.
14. **HDR Tone Mapping Modernization** – platform-calibrated HDR profiles.
15. **Realistic Shadow Cascade Pack** – improved long-range shadowing.
16. **Photomode Pro Toolkit** – exposure, lens, focus, path toggle.
17. **Cinematic Motion Blur Options** – camera/object blur profiles.
18. **Foliage Wind Interaction Pack** – biome-aware wind animation.
19. **Ocean & Water Shader Revamp** – caustics, foam, shoreline physics.
20. **Performance Auto-Tuner** – quality scaling for stable framerate.

## Suggested Delivery Waves
- **Wave 1:** Core rendering uplift + party link foundation.
- **Wave 2:** Cosmetic economy, VIP systems, voice tooling.
- **Wave 3:** UGC marketplace, VR social extensions, sponsorship stack.

## Notes
- Prioritize user trust and fair monetization: cosmetic-first, no pay-to-win.
- Include parental controls, privacy consent, and robust moderation by default.
- Ensure all Meta Horizon integrations remain optional and standards-compliant.
